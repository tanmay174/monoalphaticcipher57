Monoalphabetic Cipher is a cipher where the letters of the plain text are mapped to ciphertext letters based on a single alphabetic key. It is a one to one mapping. Substitution ciphers work by replacing each letter of the plaintext with another letter. For this reason, a monoalphabetic cipher is also called a simple substitution cipher.
